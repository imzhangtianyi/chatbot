    • About the Project

    What does it do

    This is a consulting project I built at Insight Data Science. I was consulting with a travel marketplace startup, and they wanted me to build a chatbot for their customer service.

    This chatbot can answer some FAQs regarding a specific product. As the figure below shows, it is able to answer or redirect about 30% of the questions asked from customers.

    For example, you can ask it "Is there any discount for Phantom of Opera", or "Can I get a refund for Wicked". If it cannot understand what specific product you are referring to, it will ask you what product are you looking at. If it cannot answer your question, it will need to redirect you to a human.

    How it works

    In order to make it identify frequently asked questions and realize what specific product the customer is referring to, I give this chatbot 2 NLU models. A topic understanding model and a product understanding model.



    I used LDA and Achored CorEx topic modeling to find frequently appearing topics from the chat transcripts.
